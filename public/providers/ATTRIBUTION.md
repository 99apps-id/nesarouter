# Provider logo sources

Provider names and marks are used only to identify configured upstream services.

- `kimi.svg`, `moonshot.svg`, `minimax.svg`, `baidu.svg`, `alibaba-cloud.svg`, `xiaomi.svg`, and `xai.svg` are local SVG assets from [Simple Icons](https://github.com/simple-icons/simple-icons), CC0-1.0.
- `together.svg` is the Together AI logomark from its official brand asset package.
- `runware.svg` is Runware's icon from the [official Runware Brand Center](https://brand.runware.ai/).
- `cerebras.svg` is the public Cerebras logo SVG from [Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Cerebras_logo.svg), marked PD-textlogo.

All other provider assets are existing project assets or text fallback badges. Trademarks remain the property of their respective owners.
